Join the community

IvyStart is a coliving network for people interested in technology, entrepreneurship, and innovation. We began as the first and only coliving network of college entrepreneurs within the Ivy League whose passion for big ideas, technology, and doing what they loved encouraged them to seek like-minded people who wanted to work on great things.

Coliving done differently

We believe that the relationships you build living in one of our communities are some of the most meaningful and valuable you’ll ever have. At IvyStart, we believe everyone with passion and drive deserves a strong network, just like what they would get at an Ivy League school.

Flexible and affordable

Stay for as little as one month, to as long as you need in Bed-Stuy, NYC. Membership starts at $590 per month, plus an additional $180 for utilities and events. We make it easy for you to move in or out.

Founder/CEO of Ivystart

My name is Chad Fong and I was born and raised in Seattle Washington. My inspiration to start Ivystart came after working in the Venture Capital Industry where I quickly discovered my passion for helping entrepreneurial people turn their visions into reality.
